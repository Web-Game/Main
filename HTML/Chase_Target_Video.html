  • Rules:

    1. Two player game(Using Two Dice).Each player has 5 life.

    2. Player has to set their DN (danger number) before start of the game using Get DN button and after that they have to set their target score.

    3. After rolling the dice the sum of numbers on the dice will be recorded.

    4. If the sum of numbers on the dice is not equal to DN then player will get +5 points if its equal to DN then he/she will score -2 points.

    5. Player has only one chance to roll the dice at a time.

    6. If the player fail to roll the dice within 10sec, then he/she will loose one life-line & score updated with -2 points.

    7. The player who loses all life-lineâ€™s first he/she will be looser & other player will win the game. Score is not considered in this case.

    8. The player who reach the target point first, will be the winner.
